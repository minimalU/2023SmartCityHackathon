Learn

Many patients need help understanding complex drug information. This limited understanding of prescribed medications by patients can lead to poor adherence, increasing the risk of side or adverse effects, and reduced treatment effectiveness. On the other hand, the difficulty in efficiently managing prescriptions and monitoring potential drug-to-drug interactions by doctors can compromise patient safety and treatment outcomes. Furthermore, the healthcare industry needs innovative solutions to strengthen the healthcare system, improve patient outcomes, and increase access to healthcare services.
